Topinka

Description

Topinka is the basic dish in every beer lover's inventory.

Delicious fried bread, some fresh garlic and no concern for bad smelling breath.

Ingrediens:

  • bread, preferably older
  • sunflower oil and lot's of it
  • garlic
  • beer

Steps:

  1. Open the beer and refresh yourself
  2. Put the pan on hellfire
  3. Pour generous amount of oil to the pan
  4. When the oil is hot (test with a drop of water), put the sliced bread onto the pan
  5. Wait till the one side is fried (warning: it's fast), then turn it.
  6. Refresh yourself again
  7. When both sides are ready, take the bread away from pan
  8. Cut one garlic clove and rub it onto the bread
  9. Congratulations, you have yourself a topinka!
  10. Drink the rest of the beer in celebration
